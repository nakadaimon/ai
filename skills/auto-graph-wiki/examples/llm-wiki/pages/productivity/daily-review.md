# Daily Review

A daily review is not a journal entry. It is an *audit* — a quick pass over the day's work to make sure nothing important was lost.

## Structure

Three questions, two minutes each:

- **What moved?** Tasks completed, decisions made, conversations that mattered.
- **What stalled?** Anything blocked, delayed, or quietly slipping. Note the cause.
- **What comes next?** The single most important thing for tomorrow morning.

## Capture loop

Anything unfinished at end of day either gets a deliberate "later" disposition (scheduled, deferred, archived) or gets pushed to tomorrow's top of list. Nothing remains in ambiguous limbo.

## Cadence

Done daily, the review is light — 5–10 minutes. Skipped for a week, it accumulates into something harder. The discipline is consistency, not depth.
